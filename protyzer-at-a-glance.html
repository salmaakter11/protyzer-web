PROTYZER At A Glance

Founded in 2012 with headquarters in Chengdu, Seamaty Technology Co., Ltd. specializes in the medical and veterinary diagnostics industry. We are global provider of point-of-care testing (POCT) medical equipment. Seamaty has passed the German TÜV ISO 13485:2016 international quality management system certification. We have established a standard GMP clean production workshop and Standard Inspection Laboratory, introduced a variety of imported R&D instrument and manufacturing equipment, researched and built a variety of automated production lines. Acknowledged and recognized by the Chinese government as high-tech enterprise and Gazelle Enterprise, Seamaty has registered many intellectual property and patent for inventions. Our products have acquired medical device product registration certificate and medical device CE certification. We have R&D centers in Chengdu and Shenzhen, and branch offices in Europe. Our products are exported to more than 80 countries all over the world including Europe and America.
